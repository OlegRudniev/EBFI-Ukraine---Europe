PORTRET OF THE COMPANY

Engineering Bureau Franke International is independent company for approval of material and goods quality, conditions and quantity, for technical consalting, for test service. To ensure the rapid and reliable development of an industry, modern approaches and intelligent management of systematized and reliable data are needed. Our company is dedicated to providing customers with proven, reliable and comprehensive information in the production sector. In addition to standard services in the field of inspection, employees of the organization are ready to develop and implement programs for monitoring production and technological processes at industrial enterprises.

QUALITY INSPECTION DURING THE MANUFACTURING

Engineering Bureau Franke International provide services for all types of industry. Depending on the type of equipment, EBFI can offer the following scope of inspection: • Input inspection of materials and components; • Control over the manufacturing process of equipment and its components at all stages of production (cutting, welding, machining, assembly, etc.); • Proceed visual control inspection, measurement and dimension control, laboratory testing, destructive and non-destructive testing; • Surveillance during acceptance tests; • Control of labelling, marking, packing and shipping. For each type of equipment and materials, we develop an unique Quality Control Plan based on requirements of product specifications and other customer specific requirements. EBFI offers independent and non-destructive testing services for the customer's materials such as: • ultrasonic test; • visual inspection; • magnetic powder control; • thickness measurement; • capillary control method; • dielectric method; • radiography testing. Non-destructive testing is carried out by own mobile ISO 17025 certified laboratory, and certified specialists by the special training international center with a second level of certification according to ISO 9712. Testing is carried out on mobile equipment owned by the Company, which allows to determine the location of testing at the request of the Customer. Typical materials: row material, steel products, parts after machining (shafts for power equipment, axels of rollers, tie rods, rolls of rolling mills, shafts for shipbuilding), welds, steel structures, welded pipes and pipelines, railway wheels, axles, anti-corrosion coating.
